About Me

I’m currently enrolled in a tech program called Correlation One, where I learn about software development. I always had an interest in technology as a kid, learning how to first use the computer in my typing class. Learning how the computer operates & works internally was something I wanted to learn but wasn’t sure what career path would work for me, especially since I also had other career ideas in mind.I know that I want a career where I can become financially stable in life as well as helping those in need whether its donating, volunteering, or charity-related.

Blog

I’m new to the tech scene & so far learning has been difficult yet an amazing experience. I like the coding part which reminds me of typing, well technically it is typing. I also like the problem solving aspect in this field, where you have to find the solution, in this case multiple solutions to build your code. Because of the problem solving skills in this field, my strength & quality is perseverance, despite the difficulties because theres no easy way in life without putting in the work. My goals are to become a Computer Programmer or a Software Developer with the hopes of including art, specifically digital art, to my career & possibly gaming. With this career, it’ll be a great outlet for growth and to express myself by building things I love as well as use my outlet to spread positivity to the world. I would like to work with creative individuals in the developer world as well as make the environment the safest and most comfortable for all people from different backgrounds/communities.
